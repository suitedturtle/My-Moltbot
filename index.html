Our Story

BORN FROM THE MAT

Gladius CombatWear was built by wrestlers, for wrestlers. Based out of Imperial, California, we know what it takes to show up ready — body, mind, and gear.

The gladius was the short sword carried by Roman legionaries — the weapon of the disciplined warrior who dominated through technique and will, not brute force alone. That's the spirit behind every singlet we make.

We design bold, performance singlets that hold their color, hold their shape, and hold up to the punishment of real competition. Whether you're stepping into your first youth match or fighting for a title, Gladius has you covered.
