About Calcojobs

A free, weekly-updated job board built for the people who keep California's robots and machines running.

What Calcojobs Is

Calcojobs is a free job board focused exclusively on robotics, automation, CNC machining, and calibration roles across California. Every listing is hand-curated and updated weekly so the jobs you see are current and relevant.

We built this because most large job boards are noisy — they mix unrelated roles together and bury the technical positions that skilled tradespeople and engineers are actually looking for. Calcojobs cuts through that. If you work with robots, machines, or precision measurement equipment, this site is for you.

Who It's For

  • Robotics & automation technicians — maintenance, repair, and operation of industrial robot systems
  • Automation & controls engineers — PLC programming, SCADA, system design and integration
  • CNC machinists & operators — precision machining for aerospace, defense, and medical industries
  • Calibration technicians — lab instruments, CMMs, laser trackers, ISO 9001 environments
  • Field service engineers — installation, commissioning, and on-site support across California
  • Embedded & robotics software engineers — firmware, motion planning, autonomous systems

  • Los Angeles — Hawthorne, Irvine, Oxnard, Palmdale, Fontana
  • San Diego — San Diego metro
  • Central Valley — Tracy, Sacramento, Bakersfield, Fresno, Modesto, Stockton

We're expanding coverage continuously. If you'd like to see a region or job category added, subscribe below and let us know.

How It Works

Every week we review open positions at companies hiring robotics and automation talent in California. Listings are added to the site and sent out in a weekly email digest to subscribers.

There's no account required to browse. Clicking "Apply" sends you directly to the employer's own careers page — we don't sit in between, collect your resume, or charge anyone anything. The site is supported by advertising so it stays completely free to use.
